Rarity System

Items in the game come in different rarity levels, each with unique appearances and special effects. The rarer the item, the more valuable it is for trading and the harder it is to steal!

Common

Basic anime characters that are easy to find but still valuable for beginners.

Uncommon

Slightly rarer characters that appear less frequently and have minor special effects.

Rare

Hard to find characters with noticeable visual effects and higher trade value.

Epic

Very rare characters that glow and have higher trade value.

Legendary

Extremely rare characters that are highly sought after with unique animations and special visual effects surrounding the character..

Mythic

A Mythical find in the game with incredible effects and super high trade value.

Secret

A Mythical find in the game with incredible effects that only a small majority of players own.

Limited

The rarest items in the game with incredible effects that only a extreamly low amount of players own.
